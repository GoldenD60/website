I'm a 15-year-old independent electronic music producer blending Dubstep, Future Bass, Electro Pop, and other electronic genres to create immersive soundscapes that fit any mood. Inspired by artists like oomiee, my music explores a wide spectrum of emotions, ranging from euphoric highs to melancholic depths. I've been producing since I was 11, shaping a signature sound built on powerful basslines, distorted vocals, and cinematic atmospheres. Every track I release is fully self-produced, reflecting my passion for sound design and storytelling. Even as a dedicated hobbyist, I'm constantly pushing boundaries, chasing new ways to craft tracks that resonate with people on a personal level. Whether you're looking for energy, introspection, or just something to vibe to, I aim to deliver music that sticks with you long after the last note.
