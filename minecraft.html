Add Server

Open Minecraft and click on Multiplayer -> Add  Server.
Select and copy the details on the left into Minecraft.
Once complete, click Done.





Direct Connect

Open Minecraft and click on Multiplayer -> Direct  Connect.
Select and copy the details on the right into Minecraft.
Once complete, click Join  Server.

In the Servers tab, locate GoldenD60  SMP in the Other  Server section.

Add in Minecraft

Open Minecraft and press Play -> Friends.
Scroll to the bottom until the Search  for  players is visible.
Press it and type GoldenD60SMP into the Search  gamertag box.
Click on Add.
You may now join the game back in the Friends section.
